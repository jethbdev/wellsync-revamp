Platform features

Everything your clinic needs — nothing it doesn't

Built for pharmacists, nurses, and administrators who need reliable tools without the complexity.

Real-time inventory tracking

Every medication logged as it moves — from receiving dock to patient bedside. Instant visibility, zero manual counts.

Smart expiry alerts

Proactive notifications before medications expire. Set custom lead times and automatically flag batches nearing expiry.

Demand forecasting

AI-powered predictions based on patient throughput, seasonal patterns, and historical usage.

Compliance & audit trails

Every transaction timestamped and logged. Stay audit-ready with automatically generated FIFO-compliant records.

Multi-location sync

MediStock keeps inventory synchronized across all sites in real time, with per-location controls and permissions.

EHR integrations

Connect via HL7 FHIR and REST APIs. Medications automatically deducted when prescribed — no double entry.

How it works

From chaos to clarity in three steps

1

Connect your pharmacy or clinic

Import your existing inventory via CSV or barcode scan in minutes. No IT team required.

2

Track every movement automatically

Scan items in and out at each point of care. MediStock handles batch numbers and lot traceability for you.

3

Act on intelligent alerts

Get notified about low stock, expiring batches, and reorder opportunities before they become problems.
